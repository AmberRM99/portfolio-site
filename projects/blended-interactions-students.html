What is the project?

After our previous research into the impact of covid on academic life, we felt the issue was too important to not further investigate.

Exploration and Discovery

Persona Led Research - Students

As the subject matter was equally so new, and yet also part of our lived experience, we began with mindmapping and brainstorming, to capture as many data points around the covid university experience as possible, to help narrow the research topic

Persona Led Research - Lecturers

Based on the mindmappings focus on a loss of social space became our primary focus, and we constructed a survey based on this topic. For our research, it was important to establish a baseline of before covid and how it compared to now, to properly address the impact of covid.
