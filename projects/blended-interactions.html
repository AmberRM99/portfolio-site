What is the project?

Blended Interactions is a research project ran between Edinburgh Napier University in Scotland, and Farmingdale State College in New York. It brings together design students from multiple disciplenarys, and presents a challenge to use knowledge of user experience research to solve modern problems using modern technology.
This project focussed on Online Learning, looking at the impact of covid and proposing solutions to how technology could help restore the university experience during covid.

Exploration and Discovery

Initial Potential User Interviews

Exploration began with small informal research meetings with a broad range of online banking users.

Each user was asked if they benefit from the existing round up system as well as their opinion surrounding it, as well as questions surrounding their personal saving and spending habits, and financial challenges they felt were unique to themselves.
